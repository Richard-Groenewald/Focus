How the value rolls up. The master row’s Lineage value is the time-phased sum across the whole family, with no double-count:
Original holds the contracted months of the current term (fulfilment_revenue).
Variation holds only the delta (+ uplift / − reduction) from its effective month — it overlaps the live term in time but never restates it.
Extension holds a new term phased to start the month after the current term ends — no month overlaps the original.
